### Historical Analog Method

@kpak82 employs systematic historical pattern comparison to predict future price movements based on past structural similarities. This methodology involves overlaying or side-by-side comparison of current price action against historical periods that exhibited similar technical setups.

**Core approach:**
- Identifies current technical structures (trendline breaks, moving average configurations, consolidation patterns)
- Searches historical charts for matching patterns with similar confluence factors
- Projects forward based on how the historical analog resolved
- Emphasizes matching multiple technical elements simultaneously (not just single indicators)

**Example execution (MAGS weekly):**
- Current chart: Horizontal breakout above UTL and 5/9 EMA to new all-time highs → reaching top of 2-year and 1-year channel ranges → bearish reversal and breakdown below UTL and 5/9 EMA support
- Historical analog: Earlier period (2017-2019) showing identical sequence
- Predicted outcome: Bearish continuation lower following the same pattern trajectory

**Key principle:** When current price structure closely mirrors historical pattern (breakout to highs followed by breakdown below key moving averages and trendlines), anticipate similar outcome will follow.

### Recurring Chart Patterns

**Wedge Patterns (Primary Focus):**
- **Falling Wedges (Bullish):** Converging downward-sloping trendlines indicating compression before upward breakout (SPY 5min intraday, VIX weekly)
- **Ascending Wedges (Bearish):** Upward-sloping converging trendlines signaling weakening momentum before breakdown (TSLA weekly at .618 fib)
- **Rising Wedges (Bearish):** Parabolic structures that break down sharply (MSFT daily breakdown)

**Reversal Patterns:**
- **Bearish Diamond Top:** Expanding then contracting price action at market peaks (AAPL daily at ~$311)
- **Dark Cloud Cover:** Two-candle bearish reversal pattern at all-time highs (NVDA weekly at ~$225)
- **Broadening Wedge/Megaphone:** Expanding channel pattern resolving with breakdown (AAPL daily)

**Consolidation Breakdowns:**
- **Rectangle/Box Range Failures:** 1-month consolidation ranges breaking to downside (NVDA, MAGS, META daily charts)
- **Symmetrical Triangles:** Apex compression patterns breaking down (META daily, NVDA daily)
- **Bear Flags:** Downward-sloping consolidation channels continuing prior downtrend (META weekly)

**Special Patterns:**
- **"Christmas Tree" Formation:** Parabolic vertical ascent indicating exhaustion (SMH weekly - "never play at the top of a Christmas tree")
- **Failed Breakouts:** Price briefly clearing resistance at confluence zones before sharp reversal (META daily at 100ema/channel resistance)
